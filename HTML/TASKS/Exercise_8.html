Welcome

Agrotech, or agricultural technology, refers to the use of innovative technologies in farming and other agricultural sectors to improve efficiency, yield, and sustainability. It involves integrating advanced tools like artificial intelligence (AI), robotics, and biotechnology with traditional farming to address challenges such as food security and climate change. Key applications include precision farming, smart irrigation, and data analytics to optimize resource use and enhance crop productivity.
